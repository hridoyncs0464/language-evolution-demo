Institute for Living Languages

Languages continue to evolve as communities adapt to social, cultural, and technological changes.

Every generation contributes new expressions, meanings, and communication styles that shape the future of language.

Research Interests

Our work focuses on language preservation, multilingual communication, and the role of technology in modern language development.

We also study how written and spoken traditions help maintain cultural identity across different regions of the world.
